# Credit Card Transactions Analysis

## Problem statement

You are provided with a dataset of transactions/users/orders. Use SQL to answer business questions.

## Suggested SQL questions

1. Total transaction volume and revenue by month.
2. Top 10 customers by total spending in the last 12 months.
3. Average transaction amount and median by merchant category.
4. Identify accounts with sudden spikes (>=3x median) in daily spend.
5. Fraud-like heuristics using LAG/LEAD: rapid multiple transactions from same card within 1 hour.
6. Rolling 7-day total spend per customer.
7. Customer RFM buckets (Recency, Frequency, Monetary).
8. Identify foreign-transaction heavy users and count.
